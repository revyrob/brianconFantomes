AUDIO WALK

An extraordinary adventure in the old town of Briançon. Travel through time, and visit the Vauban city in a different way. Let us frighten and surprise you as we guide you through the alleys and passages. Discover the dark history and legends of Briançon with Briançon Fantômes!

WHAT

You will be transported back in time by the stories of the Celts who settled in this region 2000 years ago, tales of the fires that have ravaged the town twice, the meeting of witches, and much more! The music and sound effects create an ambiance you won't forget. Briançon Fantômes is not for the faint-hearted!

Our mission is to entertain, scare and help you discover the history of Briançon!

You will need a smartphone, headphones, and Internet/Data.

Briançon Fantômes takes between 60-90 minutes to complete and we recommend to do it in the late afternoon or evening because the atmosphere in the old town of Briançon is mystical as dusk arrives.

By using Briançon Fantômes, you agree to the following: if you injure yourself, pass out, lose your false teeth, contract the plague, your hair turns white with dread or anything that could harm your health - will be your fault!
